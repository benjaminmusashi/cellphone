Solution Description

My solution to this problem is to use cell phones to better connect people. "Phone renters" will be placed in as many public buildings as possible. These renters will allow people to rent phones and use them how they want, but will cost $20 with a 12 hour limit (will have fees).

FAQs

Q. What is the estimated cost to carry out this solution?

 

A. It includes the iPhone 11, $500, the table, which costs around $1000, the Bluetooth Mobile Credit Card Reader, $25, and the GPS Screen, around $100.

Q. How will it work?

 

A. First, the customer will swipe his/her membership card, credit card, etc. Then, the phone is unlocked and you are allowed to take the phone. Enter in your name on the screen and it will start recording your statistics. The screen is for people to see how much time has elapsed, and who has it.

Q. Where will they be located?

 

A. Any local or public building willing to install the phones, but mostly in poor cities or towns.
