Argumentative Essay

Thesis Statement

Poverty is a huge problem all around the world. Almost 9%, or 700 million people live in poverty, according to worldvision. Cell phones can help alleviate poverty because of easy communication and access to services.

Reason 1

One reason why cell phones are helpful is because of access to services. For example, according to borgenproject.org, phones can help the poor in four ways, "literacy & education, agriculture, banking, and health." These are just some of the services that can benefit poor people.

Reason 2

Another reason why cell phones can contribute to solving poverty is because of easy communication. This communication is important because as Iqbal Quadir said, "connectivity is productivity." Citizens will benefit from this communication and improve efficiency and capacity.
